A hefty snare sculpted from bell bronze.

DW’s True-Cast snare is a family of heavy-duty, hand-built snare drums that feature a concave bell bronze shell with machined 100% brass hardware for balanced attack and dark, earthy tone.

DW MFG True-Cast Snare Drum, 5x14"

  • Sand-Cast Bell Bronze Shell

    With a 5mm to 3mm concave inner shell wall for hearty attack and enhanced low end.

  • 100% Brass Machined Hardware

    Each piece of hardware is hand machined by DW from pure brass in Oxnard, California.

  • Limited Edition

    The third generation, 5x14" True-Cast is limited to 100 units worldwide, each snare features a numbered badge and certificate of authenticity.

  • Bell bronze is back. Part # DRVZ0514STZ

    The 5x14” True-Cast snare features a sandcast and machined Bell Bronze shell that offers a contoured interior shape ranging from 5mm to the top and bottom edges to 3mm at the center. The beefy drum delivers fat, low-end punch that does not choke out at any tuning.

    1. Sand-Cast bell bronze shell
    2. Machined True-Cast counter hoops
    3. Machined Mini-Turret lugs
    4. Machined mini MAG throw-off

    DW will produce 100 of these special 5 x 14” True-Cast Snare Drums snares and each will include a signed certificate of authenticity.
