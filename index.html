The Drummer’s Choice™ since 1972.

Founded in 1972 by Don Lombardi, Drum Workshop (DW) has earned a reputation as the most respected custom drum company in the world.

DW’s innovative advancements in the tonal qualities of wood led by the unwavering creativity of John Good has revolutionized the craft of custom drums. DW hardware and pedals are trusted by the world’s best drummers and recognized as the industry standard by players of all levels.
