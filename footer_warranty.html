Drum Workshop, Inc. Limited Warranty

  • Drum Workshop, Inc. Hardware and Accessories Limited Warranty:

    For a period of Five (5) years from the original date of purchase, Drum Workshop, Inc. guarantees the original owner, when presented with proof of purchase, that all Drum Workshop, Inc. Hardware and Accessory cast parts are free of material and manufacturing defects. This warranty is limited to cast parts only, such as the tilter castings, tripod castings, tube joint castings and wing nut castings. If under normal playing conditions cast parts covered in this limited five-year warranty fail, they will be replaced at no charge. If moving parts, such as knurled rods, threaded nuts and bolts, and tripod legs, etc. fail within One (1) year from the original date of purchase, they will be replaced at no charge. Return the hardware or accessory to your authorized Drum Workshop, Inc. dealer or, if there is not a dealer in your area, contact DW directly. DO NOT send hardware or accessory to Drum Workshop, Inc. without first receiving a Return Authorization (RA) number. Shipping charges to Drum Workshop, Inc. will be paid by the consumer. Drum Workshop, Inc will not accept returns with evidence of misuse or alteration. At Drum Workshop, Inc., the maximum liability pursuant to this warranty is limited to the monetary value of the product that is the subject of the warranty claim.

Bass Drum Pedal cast parts are free of material and manufacturing defects. This warranty is limited to cast parts only, such as the base casting, footboard, beater hub, sprocket, heel and cam casting. If under normal playing conditions cast parts covered in this limited five-year warranty fail, they will be replaced at no charge. If moving parts, such as the spring assembly, beater ball, radius rod, hex shaft, ball bearings, etc. fail within One (1) year from the original date of purchase, they will be replaced at no charge. Return the pedal to your authorized Drum Workshop, Inc. dealer or, if there is not a dealer in your area, contact Drum Workshop, Inc. directly. If under normal usage, parts covered in this one-year warranty fail, they will be replaced at no charge. Return the product to your authorized DW dealer or, if there is not a dealer in your area, contact DW directly. DO NOT return the product without first receiving a Return Authorization Number.
