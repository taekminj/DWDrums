About the Performance Series™

The Performance Series is handcrafted in Oxnard, California from 100% North American Hard Rock Maple. These drums utilize DW’s groundbreaking shell construction and drum hardware to deliver you a complete instrument.

North American Maple Shells

All Performance Series™ shells are built with DW’s patented HVX™ technique, which features 100% Maple plies with grains oriented in horizontal, vertical, and diagonal patterns for low, punchy fundamentals.

Bearing Edge

HVX™ shells are precision-cut with 45-degree (toms) and 60-degree (bass and snare) edges. These edges provide tonality and sustain from your toms, and depth and punch from your bass and snare.

Customize your look.

Hardware Options

DW drum hardware keeps shells tuned and true, but they’re also available in a variety of colors to enhance the aesthetic of your customized drums.

  • LUGS / COLORS

    Performance Series™ lugs offer a quarter-sized version of the iconic turret design. All Performance Series™ hardware is available exclusively in Chrome.

    1. Chrome
  • TRUE-PITCH® TUNING RODS

    True-Pitch® tuning features 5mm threads rather than the standard 12/24. There’s a thread every 0.8mm on DW True-Pitch® rods or about 20% more threads, versus a thread every 1.05mm on standard rods. The result is a more exact tuning process. Collector's Series kits feature True-Pitch® TP50 stainless steel tuning rods.

    1. Stainless Steel TP50
  • TRUE HOOPS®

    Performance Series are available exclusively with Chrome triple-flange steel hoops to give your drums great versatile sound. These hoops come in graduated weights (1.6mm for 8 & 10" toms, 2.3mm for 12-18" toms, and 3mm for snares).

    1. Chrome
  • DW MAG™ THROW-OFF SYSTEMS

    The MAG™ throw-off has an integrated magnet and horizontal tension adjustment for smooth, easy-adjust action.

    1. Chrome
  • BUTT PLATES

    The versatile 3P butt plate offers three different levels of strainer tension.

    1. Chrome
  • STM™ - SUSPENSION TOM MOUN

    DW’s patented STM™ system allows shells maximum resonance while securing your toms in place. No washers or grommets contact the lug rod receivers, so choking is reduced, and tuning is as easy as ever.

    1. Chrome
  • FLOOR TOM LEGS

    Performance Series Tom Legs allow you to adjust the resonance of any DW floor tom by adjusting the knurled knob. Available in 21" Chrome plated steel.

    1. Chrome
