About DW Sessions

DW Sessions are created to showcase the chops of DW artists and help inspire the global community of DW drummers. DW Drums are handcrafted to perform as a complete musical instrument, so each session is presented without interviews to put the full focus on the performance of DW artists bringing DW Drums to life.
